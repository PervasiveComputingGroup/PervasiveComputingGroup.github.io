Dr Po Yang

  • Profile
    Dr Po Yang (PY) is Senior Lecturer of Large-scale Data Fusion at the Computer Science Department at TUoS. He graduated with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from Wuhan University in China in 2004, before being awarded his MSc in Computer Science from the University of Bristol in 2006. In 2010 he graduated with a PhD in Electronic Engineering from the University of Staffordshire.

    His research interests include pervasive data mining, machine learning, image analysis, and precision agriculture. He was the PI of three InnovateUK FIP projects (ID: I07462, 10002902 and 10050919), one BBSRC project (BB/X01200X/1) and one EPSRC CASE PhD project (EP/T517835/1).
